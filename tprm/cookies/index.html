Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how TPRM, operated by SD Solutions NC, uses cookies when you use our Service.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by a website. Cookies serve various functions — they can help a website remember your preferences, keep you logged in, or track how you use the site.

Cookies we use

TPRM uses one cookie only:

Cookie name Purpose Type Duration
token Keeps you logged in by storing a secure authentication token Essential / Session 7 days or until logout

This cookie is:

  • –Strictly necessary — the Service cannot function without it
  • –HTTP-only — cannot be read or modified by JavaScript
  • –Secure — only transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • –SameSite: Lax — protected against cross-site request forgery

What we do not use

  • –Advertising or targeting cookies
  • –Analytics or tracking cookies
  • –Third-party cookies of any kind
  • –Persistent tracking technologies (pixels, beacons, fingerprinting)

Your choices

Because our only cookie is strictly necessary for the Service to function, it cannot be disabled without preventing you from logging in. If you do not wish to accept this cookie, you should not use the Service.

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Deleting the token cookie will log you out of the Service.
