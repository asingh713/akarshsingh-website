2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary for the app to function. No advertising identifiers, no behavioral tracking, no analytics SDKs, and Budgy never connects to your bank or any financial institution.

Account identifiers

If you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique anonymized user identifier and, only if you choose to share it, your name and email address. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google display name and email address via Google's official Sign-In SDK. These are stored in your device's Keychain and synced privately to your iCloud account. You can also use Budgy with just a local device passcode and Face ID/Touch ID, without any sign-in.

Financial data you enter

Accounts, budgets, categories, transactions, recurring rules, and savings goals — all created and entered by you directly in the app. Budgy does not connect to any bank, card issuer, or financial data aggregator (such as Plaid). There is no automatic transaction pull from any institution.

CSV file imports

If you import a transaction file (e.g. a bank export), the file is read and processed entirely on your device. It is never uploaded to us or to any third-party server. Imported data becomes part of your local/iCloud transaction data described above.

App passcode and biometrics

Your app passcode is hashed and stored in your device's Keychain — we never see or store the plaintext passcode. Face ID / Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on-device; biometric data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us.

What we do NOT collect

Bank credentials, card numbers, account numbers, advertising identifiers (IDFA), precise location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, crash reports, usage analytics, or any device fingerprinting data.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

On your device: All financial data — accounts, budgets, categories, transactions, and savings goals — is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework in an encrypted database. Your app passcode is stored as a salted hash in your device's Keychain, protected by your device passcode and biometrics.

iCloud sync (optional): If you sign in with Apple or Google, your data syncs to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. Only you can access it. SD Solutions NC has no access to your iCloud data, no keys to decrypt it, and no servers storing copies of it.

CSV imports: Bank export files you import are parsed entirely on-device and never transmitted anywhere.

No SD Solutions NC servers: We operate no backend servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that stores your personal or financial data. We do not connect to your bank.

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active use: Data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account for as long as you use the app.
  • Sign out / data deletion: All data is permanently deleted from your device and iCloud when you delete it via Settings → Delete All Data (or equivalent). iCloud deletion typically propagates within minutes.
  • App deletion: Deleting the app removes all local data. iCloud data remains until you delete it first in-app, or via iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.
  • Keychain: Your hashed passcode is deleted when you remove app lock or delete the app. Some iOS versions may retain Keychain items after app deletion; remove these via Settings → Password & Security if needed.
