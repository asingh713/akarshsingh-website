Akarsh Singh Worky — Privacy Policy

Worky iOS App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective for all versions of Worky

Overview

Worky is a personal workout tracking app built and maintained by Akarsh Singh. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Worky collects, how it is used, and what is never collected or shared. The short version: your data stays on your device and your iCloud account — it is never sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Data Storage

All workout data, exercise logs, personal information (name, age, height, weight), and daily notes you enter in Worky are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

If you sign in with Apple or Google, your data is synced privately to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. This sync is end-to-end private — Akarsh Singh and Worky have no access to your iCloud data. We do not operate any servers or databases that store your personal information.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, your Apple ID is used solely to identify your account for iCloud sync purposes. Worky receives only a unique anonymized identifier from Apple — we do not receive your Apple ID email address unless you explicitly choose to share it during sign-in. Your Apple credentials are stored securely in your device's Keychain and are never transmitted to any third-party server.

Sign in with Google

If you choose to sign in with Google, Worky uses Google's official Sign-In SDK to authenticate your account. Your Google credentials are handled entirely by Google's own systems. Worky only receives your display name and email address to personalize your experience — this information is stored locally on your device and in your iCloud account. It is never sent to any Worky server.

Network Requests

Worky makes one type of outbound network request: exercise name searches sent to wger.de, a free open-source exercise database, when you use the exercise search feature. Only the search term you type (e.g. "bench press") is sent — no personal information, no account details, and no device identifiers are included in these requests.

No Tracking or Analytics

Worky does not track you across apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA), third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or any form of behavioral tracking. No data is ever sold to third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data at any time from within the app via Settings → Sign Out. This permanently removes all workout data, logs, notes, and personal information from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, deleting locally will also remove the data from your iCloud account. Uninstalling the app from your device will remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Worky is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact form at akarshsingh.com .
