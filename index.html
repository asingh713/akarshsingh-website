Services

Small Business Tech Consulting

Running a small business is hard work, and technology shouldn't make it any harder. I help small businesses get set up, organized, and running efficiently so owners and teams can focus on what they do best, rather than spending time troubleshooting.

I have a personal connection to small businesses. Growing up as a child of immigrants who owned and ran their own business, I witnessed firsthand the hard work and passion people pour into their dreams. Technology shouldn't be a roadblock in those dreams. That experience shaped why I do this work today: helping local businesses use technology in practical, affordable, and effective ways to make their operations simpler, more secure, and more efficient.

I work across all aspects of small business tech: setting up hardware and software, installing networking hardware and configuring networks, configuring mobile devices, and implementing cloud tools and email systems. I can help with workflow automation, integrate AI tools where they make sense, and assist with physical security like cameras, sensors, and access controls. My goal is to provide solutions that actually work for your team — nothing overly complicated, nothing unnecessary — just the right tools to help your business succeed. Whether you're starting from scratch, inheriting a messy setup, or just looking to improve how your business runs, I can help you build a setup that works today and scales for tomorrow.
