Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Cancellation

You may cancel your Vetta subscription at any time through your account settings under Billing. There is no cancellation fee.

When you cancel:

  • –Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
  • –You will not be charged again after that date
  • –Your data remains accessible until the period ends
  • –After the period ends, your data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion

Refunds

Monthly subscriptions.  We do not offer refunds for partial months. If you cancel mid-cycle, you retain access for the remainder of that period but will not receive a refund for unused days.

Annual subscriptions.  If we offer annual billing in the future, specific refund terms for annual plans will be published at that time.

Exceptions.  We may issue refunds at our discretion in the following circumstances:

  • –Duplicate charges caused by a billing error
  • –Charges made after a cancellation was requested and confirmed
  • –Extended Service unavailability exceeding 72 consecutive hours in a billing month — prorated credit or refund for the affected period

Requesting a refund.  Contact akarshsingh.com/contact with your account email and a description of the issue. Refund requests are reviewed within 5 business days.

Plan Downgrades

  • –The new plan takes effect at the start of your next billing period
  • –No credit is issued for the difference in the current period
  • –If your current usage exceeds the new plan's limits, you will be prompted to reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect

Plan Upgrades

  • –The new plan takes effect immediately
  • –You will be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of the current billing period
  • –New limits (vendor cap, AI searches) take effect immediately upon upgrade

Account Termination by SD Solutions NC

If we terminate your account for violation of our Terms of Service, no refund will be issued for any remaining subscription period.

If we discontinue the Service entirely, we will provide at least 30 days' written notice and issue prorated refunds for any prepaid period beyond the shutdown date.
